Logs And Data Usage

For us to access your pending payouts, we will need to log some imformations like the number of your posts that have not paid out provided it has a vote on it, the total amount of vote worth in dollars on your posts that have not paid out, the number of comments that have not paid out provided it has a vote on it and the value worth of the comment in $

Process of Information Collection

Our Bot Connect to your steemit account (on the steem blockchain) to get details about the votes on your posts.

Then, run a script which will link the steemjs api to bring in all their posts details including resteemed posts, then generate a table containing all the generated details in a ordere form while their sbd payouts gets added up.

Compare information for accuracy and verify it with documentations about how the steem blockchain operates.
