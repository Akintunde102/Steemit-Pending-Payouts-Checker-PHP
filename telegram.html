Why Telegram & Discord?

Please read the text below before heading to the Telegram Channel or Discord Server
We had to create a telegram channel for SteemPayout for the following reasons:
  1. We feel it would not be a bad idea to encourage discussions among members.
  2. We also felt members might want to share certain information about steem as a whole, one which could be beneficial to another user on the steempayout platform.
  3. In cases where certain user(s) find it hard to understand some features on the steempayout Platform, they could have a support community where they can ask questions and get help.
  4. Most importantly, we felt there is a need for users to have an open market place where they can make certain currency exchange.
